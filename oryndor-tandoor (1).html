Our Heritage

Rooted in the
Valleys of Kashmir

Nestled among the mist-laden valleys and sun-drenched fields of Jammu & Kashmir, Oryndor Tandoor was born from a singular conviction: that the world deserves spices as pure as the mountain air they're grown in.

We work directly with local farmers across Kashmir's most fertile regions — sourcing only the finest Kashmiri red chillies, fragrant methi, and golden turmeric. Each batch is stone-ground using traditional methods, preserving the volatile oils and deep aromas that modern machines destroy.

🌿
Farm-Direct Sourcing Partnered with over 40 small-scale farms across the Kashmir Valley for unmatched freshness and traceability.
⚙️
Traditional Stone-Grinding Cold-press stone mills preserve the full spectrum of aroma, colour, and flavour — just as nature intended.
📦
Zero Additives, Zero Compromise No artificial colours, no fillers, no preservatives. Only pure spice — guaranteed.
